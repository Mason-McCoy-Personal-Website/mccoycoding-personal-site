The Norse Runes

FEHU

ᚠ

A rune of power, representing new beginnings and wealth such as money

URUZ

ᚢ

A rune of power that we can't control, representing personal success or healing

THURISAZ

ᚦ

Also known as thorn, this rune represents the ability to resist conflict, and is an aspect of protection

ANSUZ

ᚨ

Representing stability, ansuz shows us order, indicating intellect and the divine breath of all life

RAIDHO

ᚱ

This rune allows us to focus our energy to obtain our goals "in the right place and time

KENAZ

ᚲ

A rune of knowledge, learning, and teaching. This allows us to look at situations with clarity

GEBO

ᚷ

Used to represent the honor and connection created when gifts are shared between individuals

WUNJO

ᚹ

A rune representing the balance of the world and all things in a chaotic world. Also represents fellowship

HAGALAZ

ᚺ

This rune represents hard times that are to come, but like the hailstone it stands for will melt away and pass

NAUDHIZ

ᚾ

This rune shows how our needs and wants can constrict us. It restricts our possibilities, but also containers the power to break free

ISA

ᛁ

Isa shows a halt in activity in our lives until a change is made

JERA

ᛃ

Representing the circle of life, this rune shows that we must go with the flow of nature to obtain our goals

EIHWAZ

ᛇ

Used as a magical protector and facilitator, this rune shows that in the end of one situation, we find ourselves in another

PERTHRO

ᛈ

The rune perthro shows us the uncertainties of life, representing freewill, memory, and problem solving

ALGIZ

ᛉ

A rune of protection, protecting your personal self and your property from harm

SOWILO

ᛊ

When in need of clarity, this rune sheds light on dark times like the sun shines after the night

TIWAZ

ᛏ

Promising victory and success in your efforts, but only if your cause was just to begin with

BERKANO

ᛒ

Berkano represents a new beginning, then birth of new possibilities and by extension new life

EHWAZ

ᛖ

This rune seeks to remind us that for success there must be a natural flow to the task. Good intentions result in success

MANNAZ

ᛗ

A rune to remind us that we're human, to encourage us to reach our full potential, and to gain the upper hand in disputes

LAGUZ

ᛚ

Just as water flows with the current, so to does this rune represent the flow of nature

INGWAZ

ᛜ

This rune allows us to spread our energy out far and wide. It's also a protective rune meant for our homes

DAGAZ

ᛞ

Represents the stability between opposite, light and dark, life and death. Lets the good energy flow while blocking the harmful energy

OTHALA

ᛟ

Represents wealth like Fehu(ᚠ), however, this wealth is immaterial like friendship, family, culture, and heritage.
