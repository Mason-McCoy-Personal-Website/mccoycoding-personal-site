Tiwaz

The Elder Futhark rune 'Tiwaz' (·õè) is one of the most important runes. From the Norse god of War, Tyr, Tiwaz is an incredibly important rune symbolizing justice, victory and leadership. Tyr and Tiwaz also influenced our modern culture with the day of the week Tuesday (Tyrsdagr)
