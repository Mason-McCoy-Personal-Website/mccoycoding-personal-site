About Mason's Work

Mason has been working since he turned 16, working at Little Caesars before leaving to go work at Savor Flavor Asia. Starting as a server, Mason often worked upwards of 36-40 hour weeks at Savor Flavor Asia, eventually filling multiple roles, including cashier, delivery driver, and Front of House Manager. After he work as a manager, he worked at a car dealership called Earnhardt Ford where he worked with 4-5 other coworkers to maintain and move customer vehicles within a lot for service and maintenance. Afterwards, Mason accepted a position at Amazon Logistics as an Operations Lead, assisting around 161 Amazon Delivery Stations under a developmental department.

While working in the Operations Lead position, Mason decided to begin coding as a way to alleviate some of the pressures that the job offered, and has even helped the department to make Standard Work changes. Tools he has helped develop eased the work of fellow Operations Leads allowed for more efficiency within the workplace
